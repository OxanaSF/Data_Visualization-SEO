What is data visualization?

It is the graphical representation of information and data.According Tufte, the guru in data visualization world, it "often the most effective way to describe, explore, and summarize a set of numbers - even a very large set - is to look at pictures of those numbers. Thurdermore, of all methods for analysing and communicating statistcal information, well-designed data graphics are usually the simplest and at hte same time the most powerful.

As we can read in the book Data Visualization with Python for Beginners: Visualize Your Data using Pandas, Matplotlib and Seaborn by AI Publishing "data visualization is precursor to many important processes such as Data Science, Machine learning, Business Intelegence, and Data Analytics. Data visualization is, without any doubt, one of the most important skillsets of the 21st century for variety of jobs.".
